Battery Log Viewer

This table displays battery and system statistics logged by your Python script.
Column explanations:

  • timestamp: Date and time of the log entry
  • percentage: Battery charge percentage
  • time_left_hms: Estimated time left on battery (hh:mm:ss)
  • power_plugged: Whether the charger is plugged in (True/False)
  • script_runtime_hms: How long the logger script has been running (hh:mm:ss)
  • cpu_percent: Total CPU usage (%)
  • ram_percent: Total RAM usage (%)
  • disk_percent: Disk usage (%) of system drive
  • brightness_percent: Display brightness (%) - Major battery impact
  • network_activity_mb: Network data transfer per interval (MB)
  • temperature_c: System temperature (Â°C) - Heat affects battery efficiency
  • charge_time_min: Estimated time to full charge (minutes) when plugged in
  • charge_status: Charging status (Full, Nearly Full, Fast Charging, Charging)
  • power_consumption_w: Estimated power consumption (watts)
  • battery_change_rate: Rate of battery change (% per minute)
  • top_10_processes: Top 10 CPU-consuming processes at the time (name:usage%)
